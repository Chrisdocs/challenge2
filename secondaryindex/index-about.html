Christopher Wolfe


Based out of Austin Texas, Florida born. I spend my free time developing my web design tool belt and going on adventures in nature. I often find myself between my love for the outdoors and my love of technology. Bringing them together whenever I can.

\/

I have been through a number of careers in my life. From architecture to photography, and retail management to gig worker. All of which have taught me great lessons and introduced me to a variety of concepts. These experiences have built me into a person who not only has design and creativity skills, but time and people management skills as well.

\/

I have a great belief in life, “keep it simple”. A phrase often said but hard to follow. No matter if I am a thousand miles from the closest habitation on a backpacking trip, or working on a project from my computer at home. It’s never a good idea to complicate things. I strive to bring this concept to my design, my code, and all of my endeavors.

"The ability to simplify means to eliminate the unnecessary so that the necessary may speak.” - Le Corbusier
