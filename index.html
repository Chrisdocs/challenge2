    I have been through a number of careers in my life. From architecture to photography, and retail management to gig worker. All of which have taught me great lessons and introduced me to a variety of concepts.     I have a great belief in life, “keep it simple”. A phrase often said but hard to follow. No matter if I am a thousand miles from the closest habitation on a backpacking trip, or working on a project from my computer at home. It’s never a good idea to complicate things. I strive to bring this concept to my design, my code, and all of my endeavors.
